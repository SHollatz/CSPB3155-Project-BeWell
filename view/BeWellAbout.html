About BeWell

In the midst of a global pandemic, contact tracing is more important than ever. The world was temporarily put on pause, but now that flights are once again running, we here at BeWell think that you should have the power to contribute to a more whole understanding of when and where diseases make themselves known.

Enter our tool: A web application built on the principle of citizen science!

The idea is simple: Have you been on a flight and now feel ill?

After flying, if you feel ill, simply enter your information into our database, and now anyone on that flight can compare and trace symptoms and illnesses across international borders.

This includes you! You have access to othersâ€™ data on their flights and symptoms. Our algorithms will even assign a status to effortlessly alert you the severity of illness on any given flight. Searching for answers has never been easier.

The more who self-report, the more robust our database becomes, and the easier it is to find out from where you caught that nasty bug.

More information. Better outcomes. Be Well.
